A Few Words About Us

Wah Ma Technology Sdn. Bhd. is a subsidiaries company of Wah Hong Industrial Corporation from Taiwan.
Since its founding in 1973, Wah Hong has concentrated in the production and sale of advanced material to integrate with the industry development.We continuously adopt new technology, improve manufacturing processes and reinforce reasonable management to cope with the dynamic and fiercely competitive industry.
Over the past three decades, we have successfully developed composite materials and advanced plastics to support sporting equipment, automobile and electronics industries.
